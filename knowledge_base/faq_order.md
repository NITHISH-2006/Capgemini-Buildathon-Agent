# Order and Ticket Status

## How can I track my support ticket or service order?
Once a ticket is created, you will receive a Ticket ID. You can go to the "My Tickets" section in the self-service portal to track its status in real time. The available statuses are 'open', 'in-progress', 'escalated', and 'resolved'.

## Will I get email notifications for ticket updates?
Yes, every time our support team updates your ticket, an automated email notification will be sent to your registered email address. You can configure notification preferences in the portal.
