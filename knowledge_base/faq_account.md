# Account Issues

## I am unable to log in to my Capgemini services portal.
If you are unable to log in, please ensure that your username and password are correct. If you still cannot log in, your account might be locked due to multiple incorrect attempts. In this case, please reset your password. If the problem persists, contact our IT Helpdesk at support@capgemini-buildathon.com.

## How do I update my profile information?
Navigate to the Profile Settings page after logging in. You can update your email address, phone number, and physical address there. Please note that changing your primary email address will require email verification.
