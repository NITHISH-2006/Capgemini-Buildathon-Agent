# Platform Availability and Support

## What are the support hours?
Our Intelligent Support Agent is available 24/7 to resolve tier-1 queries. Human agents are available Monday to Friday, from 9 AM to 6 PM EST. If an issue is escalated outside human working hours, it will be queued for the next business day. 

## Are there any planned maintenance windows?
Routine maintenance for the Capgemini Buildathon Portal typically occurs on Sunday mornings between 2 AM and 4 AM EST. During this time, you may experience brief interruptions. Notifications are sent a week in advance for major downtimes.
